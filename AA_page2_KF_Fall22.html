Is Anything on Social Media Private?

Introduction


The risk of having your content shared outside your trusted network is just too high for social media to ever offer a reliable sense of privacy for users.
Address the expectation of privacy

Responsibility


Reaching any level of privacy comes down to the user being responsible with what they share.
"Pause before Posting"

False Security


Features like privating your account provide false security for users. While these post's can't be shared, however users take a screenshot.
Learn about privacy settings
